# Evidence (synthetic)

> **All artifacts in this folder are fictional**, created for a portfolio AI governance
> risk assessment of the invented company **FakeOrg**. They are intentionally **incomplete**
> and contain deliberate governance gaps — they model the kind of evidence an assessor
> collects and the gaps they typically find. Nothing here is real guidance or a real document.

These artifacts are the evidence base the assessment references when scoring controls.

| File | Represents | Notable gaps (assessor findings) |
|---|---|---|
| `draft-ai-acceptable-use-policy.md` | A draft, unapproved AI policy | Not approved; no high-risk provisions; no bias-testing requirement; no inventory mandate |
| `vendor-talentsift-datasheet.md` | Vendor data sheet for the résumé-screening tool (UC-01) | No bias/fairness audit; no Annex IV technical documentation; human-oversight unspecified |
| `support-chatbot-sop.md` | Operating procedure for the support chatbot (UC-02) | Inconsistent AI disclosure; no harmful-output evaluation; no log-retention policy |
