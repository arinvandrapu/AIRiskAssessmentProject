# FakeOrg — Acceptable Use of AI Tools (DRAFT)

> **SYNTHETIC ARTIFACT.** Fictional draft policy for a portfolio assessment.

**Status:** DRAFT v0.3 — not approved · **Owner:** IT · **Last edited:** 2026-03-11
**Approval:** pending review by Legal and the (not-yet-formed) AI governance group.

## 1. Purpose
Provide guidance to FakeOrg employees on the responsible use of generative AI tools
(e.g., chat assistants, code assistants, content generators) in their daily work.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all employees and contractors using AI tools on FakeOrg systems or data.

## 3. Guidelines
- Do not paste customer personal data or secrets into public AI tools.
- Review AI-generated output before use; you remain responsible for your work.
- Use company-approved tools where available.

## 4. Prohibited
- Using AI tools to make final hiring, firing, or disciplinary decisions without review.
- Sharing confidential FakeOrg information with unapproved external services.
