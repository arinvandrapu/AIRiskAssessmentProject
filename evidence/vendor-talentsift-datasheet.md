## Overview
TalentSift AI parses inbound applications and ranks candidates against a role profile to
help recruiters prioritise review. Combines a resume-parsing LLM with a learned ranking model.

## Capabilities
- Parses CVs/resumes and extracts skills, experience, and education.
- Produces a 0–100 "fit score" and a ranked shortlist per requisition.
- Integrates with major applicant-tracking systems via API.

## Performance (vendor figures)
- Parsing accuracy: "95%+ on standard resume formats."
- Average time-to-shortlist reduced by "40%."

## Data handling

- Data Processing Agreement (DPA) available on request.
- Customer data "is not used to train shared models" (configurable).
